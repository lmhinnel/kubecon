Deploying to the real world is like taking an exam you need to pass, and for newbies to the cloud-native scene, this is especially true for application developers. It’s a big step from “code on my laptop” to “apps running at scale.” It’s gritty work—grungy Dockerfiles, constant YAML fiddling, and the horror of the dreaded pager call at midnight.

In this session, we’ll present you with a beginner’s guide to a Golden Path to help you sleep at night. Here’s a quick overview of how to combine:

- Cloud Native Buildpacks: build production-grade images without ever having to touch Dockerfile code.
- Tekton: run your CI/CD pipelines directly on Kubernetes, native to the platform.
- Knative: deploy applications with serverless ease and enjoy scaling and scale-to-zero for free.
- Observability: add observability to your system to keep issues small and easy to solve.

If you are a student or new to the field and looking to get started in your career, this session is for you and will present you with a blueprint for an automated, observable, and stress-free deployment workflow on Kubernetes.
